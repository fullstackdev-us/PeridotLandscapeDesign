The Design Process

The Design Process

After a 10–15-minute phone call discussing the project and your design needs, a follow-up site visit will be scheduled to see the scope of work. After the site visit, the work agreement and estimate will be set up and after the deposit is received, the process really begins!

I will have a questionnaire for you to filled out, going over likes/dislikes, preferred styles, colors, etc. During this time I’ll return with an assistant to measure the site and take photographs to get started on the base plan drawing. Utilities will also need to be located at this stage (411) if necessary.

Over the next several weeks we’ll meet virtually to go over rough sketch design ideas and decide on one that is the best fit.

The final meeting will be an in-person presentation of the Masterplan drawings and plant list, and the final payment for the design will be due.

Project Management

Project Management

A separate Project Management agreement will be set up. The price for my service is generally 7-10% of the cost of the project and varies depending on the amount of involvement required. If you already have a landscaper you know and would like to use, the PM fee is lower.

Project Management Includes:

  • Arranging installation contractor (unless already arranged)
  • On-site visits during installation
  • Procurement of plants
  • Hand-placing plants according to the design and assisting with planting
  • One follow-up visit 30-60 days post installation
