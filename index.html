Landscape Design

The Design Process

We’ll begin with a 10–15-minute phone call discussing the project and your design needs. A follow-up site visit will be scheduled to see the scope of work. I will have a detailed questionnaire for you to fill out, going over likes/dislikes, preferred styles, colors, etc. This is a vital part of the process and will help me design a space that is comfortable and pleasing to you. After the site visit, the work agreement and estimate will be established, and if approved, the process really begins!

I’ll return with an assistant to measure the site, take many photographs, and get started on the base plan drawing and site analysis. Utilities will also need to be located at this stage (811) if necessary.

Over the following weeks, we’ll meet virtually to go over the site conditions/requirements, a few rough sketched design ideas, and decide on one that is the best fit. This process can take up to 30 days.

The final meeting will be an in-person presentation of the Masterplan drawings and plant list, and the final payment for design services will be due.

Project Oversight

Project Oversight

A separate Project Oversight agreement will be set up. The price for this service is generally 7-10% of the cost of the project and varies depending on the amount of involvement required.

Project Oversight Includes:

  • Arranging installation contractor (unless already arranged)
  • On-site visits during installation
  • Procurement of plants
  • Hand-placing plants according to the design and assisting with planting
  • Three follow-up visits, at 30, 60 and 90-days post installation, to make sure things are going well
